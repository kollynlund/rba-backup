Royal Business Academy (RBA) is an intensive business training program where participants learn proven principles for launching and growing a successful business. The program is designed for individuals with an entrepreneurial mindset and includes online learning and a 4-week residential program in the Czech Republic.

Participants will:

  • Study and learn to apply key business management tools and principles that have significance in today's rapidly changing environment.
  • Develop skills in critical thinking and problem solving.
  • Strengthen their presentation, communication, and marketing skills.
  • Develop leadership skills needed to manage their own company.
  • Gain an appreciation of business ethics and the importance of personal integrity.
  • Establish a personal network by developing lasting relationships with peers and mentors, and by learning new networking skills.
  • Acquire entrepreneurial skills while developing and executing a viable business plan for a new or existing business.
  • Spend one-on-one time with successful business owners.
  • Become innovative and find solutions to real world problems.
  • Identify immediate personal application and apply lessons leaarned.
  • Increase their creativity.
  • Break away from confining personal barriers.
  • Define their professional vision, set SMART goals, and be accountable.
  • Receive step-by-step instructions on finding a business idea, validating assumptions, and growing a business.
  • No longer look at providing for their family as an impossible or stressful task.
  • Learn how to stay motivated through hard times.

About the Cost
The real cost for us to operate this program is significantly higher than the tuition you are asked to pay. However, we want the program to be available to EVERY young man and young woman who wishes to become an outstanding leader in their community. How can we afford to do this? We believe that if you follow the principles we teach, your business will be successful. In exchange for your ability to attend this program at a reduced price, we invite you to offer us a small amount of ownership in the business you create during the program. This way, our success is dependent on helping you be as successful as possible.

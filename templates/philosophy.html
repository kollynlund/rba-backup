Mission Statement

Royal Business Academy strengthens families and communities by providing business education and mentoring to young adults throughout the world and by helping them establish and grow their own successful businesses.

Our goal is to attract young men and young women of character and professional ability who wish to -

  • Become outstanding leaders in their communities
  • Learn new business skills from experienced and successful mentors
  • Use the opportunity to start or grow their own business
  • Learn skills that will help them develop and strengthen their role as a leader in their family, community, and profession

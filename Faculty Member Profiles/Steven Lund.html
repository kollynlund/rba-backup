Steven Lund

MENTOR

Steven Lund has over 30 years of experience in sales, marketing, and business development. He has funded and launched four technology companies and is currently the CEO of SourceThought, a big data and analytics company focused on preparing data for analysis. Prior to SourceThought, Steven was the World Wide Director of Business Development for Cisco Services, President of Primex Wireless, and Chief Operations Officer at Resilient Networks. He has also held senior business development, sales, and product management positions at HP and Apple, where he directly influenced many of the new products Apple brought to market. Steven holds a Masters from University of Utah and a Bachelor of Science in Business Management/Marketing from Brigham Young University with minors in Economics and Accounting. Steven has served as an advisor to BYU’s Marriott School of Management for the Center of Entrepreneurship, Thanksgiving Point Children’s Museum of Natural Curiosity and several startups. He speaks Spanish, has lived in Spain, traveled extensively internationally, and as the father of six sons, enjoys golfing, water skiing, snow skiing, mountain biking, and hiking.

Spencer Rogers

FOUNDER

Spencer Rogers is a founder of Royal Business Academy and serves as its General Manager and Course Director. He is a skilled teacher and knows how to present complex business concepts in a simple and easy-to-understand way. Spencer is a seasoned entrepreneur with over 20 years of professional experience building businesses and mentoring others. He has founded eight successful companies, raised more than $15 million in working capital, and successfully negotiated multiple business exits including an 8-figure asset sale and the sale of two technology companies. In addition to his duties at Royal Business Academy, Spencer serves as Associate Director of Technology Transfer at Brigham Young University where he is responsible for working with businesses of all sizes to commercialize the University’s inventions. He also owns and operates several businesses including QuickStove and SR Consulting. Spencer holds four United States patents, speaks three languages, and has worked and traveled extensively throughout the world. He is happily married and is the proud father of seven children.

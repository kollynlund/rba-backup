Tom Peterson

MENTOR

Tom Peterson is a Professor of Entrepreneurship in the Marriott School of Management at Brigham Young University where he teaches MBA courses on entrepreneurship and commercial real estate finance and development. Tom is an extremely popular professor and is well-liked by his students. Prior to joining Brigham Young University, Tom served as a Wyndham Hotel Company Development Partner and as Chief Financial Officer, and as a Principal at Trammell Crow Ventures Real Estate Investment Banking. Tom has owned two dozen companies and has served as a member of the Board of Directors for nine companies in the past 25 years. He is an expert in small business ideation, business model validation, business formation and governance, financial forecasting, and other areas related to launching new business ventures. Tom received a BA in English with an Economics minor from Brigham Young University, and he received an MBA from Harvard Business School in Boston, MA.

All Instructions: Please email the items below to apply@royalbusinessacademy.org

Photo Upload

Please email us a photograph of that can be used for our program directory. The photo should be a close-up of your head and shoulders and should be at least 500 pixels tall and 500 pixels wide.

Please email the photograph file in '.jpg' format.

Curriculum Vitae

Please send us a summary of your professional and educational background. Make sure to include the following:

  • Your name, address, phone number, and email address
  • At least 6 keywords that best describe your professional strengths or abilities
  • Names, dates, and a brief description of each of your prior jobs
  • Names and dates of all colleges, universities or other post-secondary institutions or programs attended

Please email the Curriculum Vitae file in '.pdf' format.

Personal Biography

Write an autobiographical statement to be shared with others in the program. Please do not exceed one page. Please include the following information:

Paragraph 1:

  • Your full name
  • Your age and month of birth
  • Your city and country of residence
  • Your primary language and any other langauges you speak
  • Your marital status -- single, married, divorced (if you have children, please tell us how many)
  • Interesting details about you and your life, such as what makes you unusual and what others would like to know about you if they knew to ask

Paragraph 2:  Describe one of your greatest accomplishments. Please include details about your specific actions.

Paragraph 3:  Describe a life-changing or course-altering experience in your life. Please provide details about what happened and how it influenced you. Sometimes such experiences are personally important but not public -- people around us may not even know what is happening.

Please email the Personal Biography file in '.pdf' format.

Personal Reference

Please email the Personal Reference file in '.pdf' format.

Professional Reference
